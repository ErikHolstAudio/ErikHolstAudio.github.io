Hi, my name is Erik. I'm a graduate of Kristiania University College and University of Inland Norway. However, more importantly, I'm a forever student of so many things.

As a passionate Sound Designer with 7+ years of experience of creating sound for different media and 4+ years of experience of programming for different applications, for games and software. I am skilled at creating memorable sounds from effects to voice over, and writing clear, concise code that is easy to maintain and troubleshoot. I have experience in working with small and large teams across multiple projects and companies. I'm able to work independently via remote locations or office environments, as needed by the company.

  • As an audio programmer I enjoy building creative tools for the audio sensory.

  • As a technical audio designer I love crafting interactive and spatial audio experiences.

  • As a sound designer I specialise in field recording and environmental sound design.
